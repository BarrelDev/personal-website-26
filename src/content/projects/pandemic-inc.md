## Overview

Pandemic Inc. is an educational game developed as a submission for the 2020 Congressional App Challenge. Built with Python and Pygame, the game simulates the spread and management of a pandemic, challenging players to make strategic decisions to contain outbreaks. Created during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic, the project aimed to raise awareness about public health measures through interactive and engaging gameplay.
