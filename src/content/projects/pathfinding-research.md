## Overview

This research project, conducted in collaboration with Dr. Wu from the New Jersey Institute of Technology, investigates and benchmarks pathfinding algorithms operating in 3D voxel space. The paper evaluates the performance characteristics of various search strategies when applied to three-dimensional grid environments, analyzing trade-offs between optimality, computational cost, and memory usage. The findings were published and presented at an IEEE conference.
