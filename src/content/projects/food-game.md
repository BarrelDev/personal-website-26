## Overview

Food Game is a work-in-progress restaurant simulation game built with C++ and the Raylib graphics library. Players take on the role of a chef, crafting dishes from various ingredients to satisfy incoming customer orders. The game features a recipe-based crafting system where ingredients are combined to create meals, with scoring based on accuracy, speed, and customer satisfaction. Development is ongoing with new recipes and mechanics being added over time.
