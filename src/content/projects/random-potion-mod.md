## Overview

Random Potion Mod is a Minecraft modification built with the Fabric modding framework. It adds a chaotic twist to gameplay by periodically applying random potion effects to the player, creating unpredictable and entertaining scenarios. The mod hooks into Minecraft's effect system and supports the full range of vanilla potion effects, from beneficial buffs like speed and strength to harmful debuffs like poison and slowness.
