## Overview

Ichbiah is the software for Team 3637's (the Daleks) 2025 FIRST Robotics Competition robot. Building on the experience from the previous season's world championship run, the codebase introduces ROS 2 integration alongside the traditional WPILib framework. This hybrid architecture enables more advanced perception and planning capabilities, allowing the robot to leverage ROS 2's ecosystem for sensor fusion and autonomous decision-making while maintaining the real-time control guarantees of WPILib.
