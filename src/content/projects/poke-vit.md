## Overview

Poke-ViT is a computer vision project that leverages the Vision Transformer (ViT) architecture to classify images of Pokemon. The model was fine-tuned on a curated dataset of Pokemon sprites and fan art, achieving strong accuracy across hundreds of different species. The project explores how transformer-based architectures, originally designed for natural language processing, can be effectively applied to image classification tasks.
