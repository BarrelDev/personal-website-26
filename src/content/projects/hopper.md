## Overview

Hopper is the software powering Team 3637's (the Daleks) 2024 FIRST Robotics Competition robot, Dalek Floyd. The codebase includes autonomous routines, teleoperated control systems, and sensor integration built on top of the WPILib framework. The robot competed at the world championship level, showcasing reliable path planning, precise mechanism control, and robust driver-assist features developed throughout the competition season.
