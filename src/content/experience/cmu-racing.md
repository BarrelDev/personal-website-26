As a driverless path-planning developer on Carnegie Mellon Racing, I work on the autonomous systems that guide an all-electric race-car around the track. My responsibilities include implementing SLAM algorithms for localization and mapping, as well as developing trajectory planning systems that enable the vehicle to navigate courses safely and efficiently. I leverage ROS 2 for real-time inter-process communication across the car's software stack.
