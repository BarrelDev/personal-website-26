As President of The Daleks, FRC Team 3637, I led the team through all phases of competition robot development, from mechanical design and fabrication to software and autonomous systems. Under my leadership, the team won a district event, qualified for the FIRST World Championships in Houston, and received the prestigious FIRST Impact Award recognizing sustained excellence in community outreach and team culture. I personally contributed to the programming effort, writing C++ and Python code for perception and path-planning systems using LiDAR and ROS 2.
