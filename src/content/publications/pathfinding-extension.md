This research introduces novel extensions to classical pathfinding algorithms, specifically A* and Dijkstra's, to handle environments where movement speeds are not fixed but randomly determined. By modeling speed variability within 3D voxel-based environments, the proposed modifications enable more realistic and efficient path planning in dynamic systems where conditions change unpredictably.
